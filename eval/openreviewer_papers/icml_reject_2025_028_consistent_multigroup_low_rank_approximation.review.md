# Review

## Summary
The authors introduce a new type of PCA that seeks to find singular vectors that minimize the maximum reconstruction error over groups of the data, while also having the property that any subset of the singular vectors also has this property. They show how to solve this problem for the case of two groups, and provide heuristics for more than two groups. They provide experiments on real data that show the effectiveness of their approach.
