While the authors prove some properties of the sliced normal distribution, they do not provide a comprehensive set of properties. For example, it would be useful to know how the sliced normal distribution behaves under common operations such as rotations and translations. Second, the numerical experiments are limited in scope. The authors only provide a few small-scale experiments, which do not fully demonstrate the potential of the sliced normal distribution.
