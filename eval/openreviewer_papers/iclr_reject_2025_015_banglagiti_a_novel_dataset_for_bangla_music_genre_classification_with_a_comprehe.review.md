# Review

## Summary
This paper presents a novel dataset for classifying Bengali music genres. The authors propose a comprehensive analysis using traditional ML, DL, and ensemble methods. The dataset includes 1,410 audio files across six genres, with features such as MFCCs, ZCR, RMS, chroma, tempo, and spectral bandwidth. The authors employ Decision Trees, Random Forests, Gradient Boost, and KNN for ML classification. For DL, they use pre-trained CNN-based models like DenseNet, ResNet, and VGGNet, achieving up to 96% accuracy. The ensemble models combine ML and DL strengths, reaching nearly 80% accuracy. The paper contributes a valuable resource for Bengali music classification and aims to enhance understanding of Bengali music through machine learning.

## Strengths
1. The paper introduces a new dataset for Bengali music genre classification, which is a valuable resource for the research community.
2. The authors provide a comprehensive analysis by employing both traditional ML and advanced DL techniques, as well as ensemble methods, to demonstrate the effectiveness of their approach.
3. The paper includes detailed experimental results, showcasing the performance of different models and their ability to classify Bengali music genres effectively.

## Weaknesses
1. The paper's focus on a specific language and cultural heritage limits its broader applicability and generalizability.
2. The paper primarily compares traditional ML and DL methods, lacking a comprehensive comparison with state-of-the-art techniques in music information retrieval.
3. The authors do not provide a detailed analysis of the proposed models' performance across different types of Bengali music or specific characteristics of the data.

## Questions
1. How does the proposed dataset compare to existing datasets for music genre classification in terms of size, diversity, and quality?
2. Can the authors provide more insights into the specific characteristics of Bengali music that pose challenges for classification?
3. How does the proposed ensemble method perform compared to other recent advancements in music genre classification?
