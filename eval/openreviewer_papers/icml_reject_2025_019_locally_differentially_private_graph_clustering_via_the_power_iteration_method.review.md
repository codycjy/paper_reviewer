# Review

## Summary
This paper proposes a private graph clustering algorithm based on the power iteration method. Compared with the previous algorithms for graph clustering, the proposed algorithm is more memory-efficient and can handle graphs with a large number of nodes. The authors also provide theoretical analysis to prove the accuracy of the proposed algorithm. Experimental results on both synthetic and real-world datasets demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed algorithm.

## Weaknesses
