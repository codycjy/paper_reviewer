## Summary
The paper extends the work of Lee and Valiant (2022) on optimal sub-Gaussianity of mean estimation to heavy-tailed distributions and contamination models. In particular, the paper proves that the estimator of Lee and Valiant is robust to $\eta$-fraction of data corruption in the strong contamination model and has optimal estimation error $O(\sigma\sqrt{\eta})$. The paper also shows that the estimator is optimal for distributions with finite $z^{th}$ moment for $1\leq z\leq 2$ and that it is asymptotically normal and efficient.
