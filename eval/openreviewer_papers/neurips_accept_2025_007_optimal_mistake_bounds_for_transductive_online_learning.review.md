# Review

## Summary
The paper studies transductive online learning where the learner receives a sequence of unlabeled instances and labels for the instances are provided online by the adversary. The main result of the paper is an optimal mistake bound of $\Theta(\sqrt{d})$ where $d$ is the Littlestone dimension of the concept class. The paper improves the previous lower bound of $\Omega(\log \log d)$ by Ben-David et al. (1995) and the previous upper bound of $\frac{2}{3}d$ by Ben-David et al. (1997).
