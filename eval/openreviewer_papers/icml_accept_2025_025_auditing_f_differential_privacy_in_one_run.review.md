# Review

## Summary
This paper proposes an auditing procedure for f-DP, which can be used to audit a wider range of privacy mechanisms. The proposed procedure requires only a single run of the mechanism and is more accurate than previous auditing procedures for DP. The authors demonstrate the effectiveness of their approach on both simple Gaussian mechanisms and models trained with DP-SGD, showing that their procedure can achieve tighter privacy estimates.

## Strengths

## Weaknesses
- The authors could provide a more detailed comparison with the existing work (Steinke et al. 2023) to highlight the novelty and advancement of the proposed method. For example, the authors could discuss the key differences and similarities between the two methods, and provide a more detailed comparison of their performance and accuracy.
- The authors could provide a more detailed discussion of the limitations of the proposed method, such as the assumptions made and the potential biases or errors that may be introduced.
